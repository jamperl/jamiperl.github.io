Dawn has been at the intersection of people and fashion for as long as she can remember. Born and raised in Toronto, Dawn has always stayed true to her roots. After a relocaiton to the United States, Dawn is back to help with all your personal needs. 3 moves, 4 closets and 2 countries later, she has all the experience you need for your home.
